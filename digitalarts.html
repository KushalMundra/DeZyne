Suryansh Nikhra

Real heroes? They Don't don't have superpowers, all they have is an amazingly appreciable will to act. The present circumstance has confined the human life form to their homes, is unable to carry out the so-important social interaction and is also living in fear of the COVID-19 virus. Not all of us have the courage to go out, stand up and work 'for the people'. Yet, there are those who are depicted in this art who are brave enough to help and provide as much support as possible; to help other people carry out their usual activities. Even though there is nothing "usual" about any of the activities we currently engage in. We are forever grateful for their contributions and owe our lives and happiness to them
- Sheersha Gupta

Job K Sebastian

Are we really free? We celebrate Independence day on 15 August every year, remembering those who struggled to free us from the clutches of the British but are we truly free now? Maybe from the British but what about our fears. Don't what others think constantly affect our decisions? We feel like our every action is being watched, judged, passed opinions on. Our actions revolve mostly around what others expect us to do, not what we want to do. We are all so desperate to 'fit in' that no one has the courage to stand out and be different. So, I ask again, are we truly free?
- Mahek Garg

We know it's futile , we know it makes us anxious. Yes, we are constantly fretting, yes we do have sleepless nights. We often start with the question "What am I wearing tonight" and promptly find ourselves contemplating the meaning of this human life. We agree we have flaws, but then again, very little astonishes us, very little bothers us, very little breaks us because at the end of the day, there's very little we haven't thought about and lived through in the vastness of our minds.
From one overthinker to another, we'll be fine.
